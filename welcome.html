Modmail

Modmail is a simple bot for handling staff support requests for a Dsicord server. It uses Discord's existing threads and adds a more user-friendly interface for starting a new one, with the intent of getting in touch with moderators or other support staff.

The bot works entirely by Discord's interactions system. This means that the bot does not read any messages from the server, and instead functions entirely passively.

Add Modmail to your server!
